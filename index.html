Process

Three steps to
unbroken focus.

01

Sensors Collect
Biometric Data

GSR, heart rate, and motion sensors built into a lightweight wearable continuously monitor your physiological state in real time.

02

AI Analyzes
Attention Levels

The ESP32 streams data to our mobile app where a machine learning model interprets patterns and scores your focus state with precision.

03

App Delivers
Smart Feedback

Personalized nudges, gamified rewards, and session insights keep you accountable and train your brain to sustain deeper focus over time.
